✅ ROLE CLASSIFICATION VERIFIED
=============================================================================

Role Mapping (Old → New):
  Speaker → Active (>60% participation)
  Listener/Engaged → Moderate (30-60% participation)
  Peripheral/Isolated → Passive (<30% participation)

Criteria:
  • Active: High gesture, dominance, central position
  • Moderate: Good engagement, responding, facing group
  • Passive: Minimal gesture, on periphery, quiet

=============================================================================
✅ METRICS CALCULATED VERIFIED
=============================================================================

Participation Level:
  Formula: Mean of all student participation scores
  Range: 0.0 (no engagement) to 1.0 (max engagement)
  Use: Overall classroom engagement indicator

Discussion Balance:
  Formula: 1.0 - variance(scores) [normalized]
  Range: 0.0 (dominated) to 1.0 (perfectly balanced)
  Use: Inclusivity and participation distribution

Most Active Student:
  Definition: Student ID with highest participation_score
  Use: Identify opinion leaders and engaged participants
